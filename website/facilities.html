Indoor / Outdoor

We’ve worked hard to create a destination that is fresh, focused and adaptable, mirroring your needs. Constantly looking to evolve, we always aim to be at the forefront of modern conveniences that range from the simple practicalities of parking through to your more complex on-demand needs.

To create a world that flows around you rather than being moulded to ours. So if your looking to enjoy a quick morning workout, an intensive evening class or simply take advantage of a space for you to meet and hang out in an exclusive, uncrowded environment we’ve got just the thing for you:

  • Time to chill out leisurely in our vast landscaped gardens.
  • Over a 100,000 sq ft, inside & out, for you to workout in style.
  • An Olympic sized pool for a daily dip or an intensive aqua workout.
  • 14,000 square foot cross fit area to help keep you focused & in your zone.
  • Outdoor running track & 30,000 sq ft training arena, for healthy fun in the sun.
  • Cycling, boxing & HIIT purpose built studios with the most advanced equipment.
  • A health orientated restaurant that offers the perfect place to eat and meet.
  • Easy access for all and dedicated reserved parking to make life simpler.
  • Industry leading coaches, professional athletes & an Olympian or two.
  • Indoor yoga studio & outdoor yoga lawn to be at one with your self.
  • A kids splash pool to help us find the next Michael Phelps.

Tailored Classes

Our classes are a step above the average. Each one has been specially designed to reflect our drive towards Macro Health™. They tune into new ways to achieve better results from exercises that are familiar yet different.
